BANKING FAQ

Q: What is a personal loan?
A: A personal loan is an unsecured loan for personal use like medical, travel or education. Interest ranges from 10.5% to 24% per annum.

Q: What is the interest rate for personal loans?
A: Personal loan interest rates range from 10.5% to 24% per annum depending on credit score and income.

Q: What is a credit card?
A: A credit card lets you borrow money for purchases and repay later. Interest is 2.5% to 3.5% per month on outstanding balance.

Q: What is a home loan?
A: A home loan is a secured loan to buy or construct property. Rates range from 8.5% to 10.5% per annum with up to 30 year tenure.

Q: What is EMI?
A: EMI is Equated Monthly Installment - fixed monthly payment covering both principal and interest on a loan.

Q: What is a savings account?
A: A savings account lets you deposit money safely and earn interest of 2.5% to 7% per annum.

Q: What is a fixed deposit?
A: A fixed deposit locks money for a fixed period at a fixed interest rate of 6.5% to 8% per annum.

Q: What is KYC?
A: KYC means Know Your Customer - mandatory identity verification using Aadhaar, PAN and address proof.

Q: What is UPI?
A: UPI is Unified Payments Interface for instant money transfers between bank accounts via mobile.

Q: What is the UPI transfer limit?
A: Daily UPI transfer limit is Rs. 1 lakh per day for most banks.
