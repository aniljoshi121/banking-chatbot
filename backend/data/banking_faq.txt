PERSONAL LOANS
A personal loan is an unsecured loan given by banks without collateral.
Interest rates: 10.5% to 24% per annum depending on credit score.
Maximum tenure: 5 years. Minimum amount: Rs 50,000.

CREDIT CARDS
Interest rate on unpaid balance: 2.5% to 3.5% per month (30% to 42% annually).
Minimum payment due: 5% of outstanding balance.
Late payment fee: Rs 750 to Rs 1200 depending on outstanding amount.

HOME LOANS
Interest rates: 8.5% to 10.5% per annum.
Maximum tenure: 30 years. Maximum loan: Up to 80% of property value.
Processing fee: 0.5% to 1% of loan amount.

SAVINGS ACCOUNTS
Minimum balance required: Rs 1,000 to Rs 10,000 depending on branch.
Interest rate: 2.7% to 3.5% per annum.
Free ATM withdrawals: 5 per month at own bank ATMs.
